LEADERSHIP

Guiding the Institution with Vision & Excellence

Inspired by strong values and dedicated leadership, St. George's College continues to nurture future-ready graduates through academic excellence and holistic development.

Founder & Secretary

Dr. G.K. Francis

With a vision to provide quality higher education and create opportunities for students from all backgrounds, Dr. G.K. Francis has been instrumental in shaping the institution's growth and academic excellence. His commitment to education, discipline, and social responsibility continues to inspire generations of learners.

Principal

Dr. V. Victor Solomon

Under the leadership of Dr. V. Victor Solomon, the college strives to foster innovation, academic excellence, and student-centered learning. His dedication to holistic education empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and values required to succeed in a rapidly evolving world.
